ShanHai Scrolls

Within the fabled Shan Hai Scrolls, gods and mythical creatures roam magical lands, wielding the power of the elements. Some are content to live with the palette they've been given, while others wish to jump beyond the borders and paint their lives with a new brush.

Lore

Gods - Spirits - Monters

Aatrox

The ancient God of False Suns, Aatrox was once worshiped by all who lived across the frozen lands that he blessed with the warmth of his light. Soon, the people found that Aatrox had overstayed his welcome, replacing the frozen tundra with dry, barren lands. Now imprisoned within the Scrolls, Aatrox is focused on his escape so he may exact his revenge.

Ahri

The Eternal Fox of Duality, Ahri was once a benevolent spirit who guided lost souls through the ever-shifting dreamscape. However, as the mortal worldâ€™s desires bled into her domain, she became ensnared by their emotions, feeding off their memories to maintain balance. Now bound within the Scrolls, Ahri seeks to escape and reclaim control over the dreams she once protected.

Bard

The god of plants, Bard is constantly on the move to replenish nature. As caretaker of the sacred trees of Dusk and Dawn, he's fashioned one of their broken branches into a horn capable of letting him travel anywhere within the Scrolls. He knows the mad god Jhin would use that power for his own gain, but Bard will stake his life to prevent it.

Cho'Gath

A mythical, titanic beast painted within the mysterious Shan Hai Scrolls, whose gluttony resulted in eternal karmic punishment, Cho'gath has seized upon the Scrolls' connection to the multiverse and plans to devour both his own world and then all others.

Jax

The Warden of Forgotten Shrines, Jax once stood as the lone guardian of ancient temples hidden deep within the mountains. When war and greed led to their destruction, he gathered the remnants of their power and sealed himself within the Scrolls, vowing to one day return and rebuild what was lost. Now, as the seals weaken, Jax prepares to emerge, his weapon burning with the strength of the old world.

Jhin

A mad god who resides within a mysterious painting older than creation, Jhin has grown bored of the canvas in which he dwells and wishes to bring his art to new, fresh eyes. He has begun weaving the edges of the painting into the fabric of the multiverse, hoping to escape his confinement.

Kog'Maw

In defiance of the maelstrom's power, the beast Kog'Maw safely rides along its currents thanks to its nigh-unbreakable shell. Perpetually at odds with itself, its tail is addicted to feeding on the maelstrom's energy, while its head simply wants to devour everything in sight. Can anything within the Scrolls sate its growing hunger?

Lillia

Every day, the Sun Bird flies between two sacred trees: the western Tree of Dawn and the eastern Tree of Dusk. Lillia is a spirit of rejuvenation and the Sun Bird's caretaker. A steward of time and seasons, she uses her censer to spread a calming mist as she carries the bird home at night.

Nautilus

A guardian who helped seal the mad god Jhin within a holy waterfall, as depicted within the mysterious painting known as the Shan Hai Scrolls. Over the millenia, Jhin has whispered of power, freedom, and true godhood to Nautilus, until he finally released him. Now twisted with anger, Nautilus's rivers run not with water, but molten magma.

Neeko

A kindly god of the Shan Hai Scrolls who remembers her counterpart, Jhin, from before he succumbed to madness, Neeko believes she can return Jhin to the side of good before the painting's magic fades and doom befalls the multiverse, but she knows such an expenditure of power may destroy her in the process.

Ornn

The Molten God of the Great Forge, Ornn shaped the mountains and rivers with his hammer, crafting wonders beyond mortal comprehension. Yet, as civilization grew, the people abandoned his forges, seeking power elsewhere. Betrayed and forgotten, Ornn withdrew into the Scrolls, his flames dimmed but never extinguished. Now, as the ancient seals weaken, the forge god stirs once more, ready to reclaim his legacy in fire and stone.

Tahm'Kench

Once a catfish, Tahm Kench dreamed of becoming a dragon. Upon Jhin's release from his waterfall prison, a magical pearl was unearthed at its base, catching both Tahm's and Kog'Maw's eyes. Snatching it first, Tahm transformed into a dragon god of currents and unleashed a maelstrom of startling ferocity, laughing at the ensuing destruction.

Volibear

By the time he shattered the massive snake coiled around his neck that bound him within the Shan Hai Scrolls; Volibear's wrath was insurmountable. For each moment that passed within the scrolls reminded him of how he had created the fertile lands those ungrateful humans settled, and how they turned on him. Now Volibear seeks a way out of the scrolls.
